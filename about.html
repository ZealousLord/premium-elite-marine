Our Background

Crafting Maritime Excellence Since 2009

Premium Elite Marine Services LLC was founded with a singular vision: to deliver world-class marine services tailored to the high standards of the UAE's luxury maritime industry. What began as a small team of passionate engineers has grown into a full-service marine solutions company trusted by yacht owners, charter fleets, and maritime businesses across Dubai and the wider UAE.

Our team of certified marine engineers, technicians, and craftsmen bring decades of combined experience across all aspects of yacht care — from precision engine diagnostics to stunning full refits. We invest in the latest equipment, materials, and training to ensure every project we undertake delivers exceptional results.

Based in Dubai Marina, we operate across all major UAE marinas and are ready to come to your vessel wherever it is berthed.

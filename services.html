What We Offer

Our Marine Services

15 premium services covering every aspect of yacht care, maintenance, and marine engineering in Dubai.

Complete Coverage

From Routine to Extraordinary

Every service performed by certified professionals using premium materials and industry-leading techniques.

01

Yacht Maintenance

Scheduled and preventive maintenance programs covering all vessel systems. We protect your investment and ensure peak performance all season long.

02

Yacht Cleaning

Thorough interior and exterior cleaning using marine-grade products. Teak scrubbing, stainless polishing, bilge cleaning, and complete wash-downs.

03

Engine Repair

Expert diagnostics, servicing, and repair of all marine engines — inboard, outboard, petrol, diesel, and hybrid propulsion systems.

04

Electrical Systems

Full marine electrical diagnostics, rewiring, shore power systems, battery banks, inverters, solar integration, and generator servicing.

05

Plumbing Services

Marine plumbing repairs, freshwater system servicing, sea-cock maintenance, holding tank services, and watermaker installation.

06

Fiberglass Repair

Structural fiberglass repair, osmosis treatment, blister repairs, gelcoat restoration, and hull laminate reinforcement by specialist craftsmen.

07

Painting & Polishing

Full hull painting, antifouling application, topside refinishing, and high-gloss machine polishing for a flawless, showroom finish.

08

Yacht Detailing

White-glove interior and exterior detailing — leather conditioning, teak treatment, chrome polishing, and full cosmetic restoration.

09

AC Repair & Maintenance

Marine HVAC system servicing, refrigerant recharging, compressor repair, and full climate control system upgrades for year-round comfort.

10

Navigation Systems Setup

Installation and calibration of chartplotters, VHF radios, AIS transponders, radar, autopilots, and integrated navigation suites.

11

Safety Equipment Installation

Life raft servicing, EPIRB installation, fire suppression systems, flare kits, smoke detectors, and full maritime safety compliance checks.

12

Yacht Refitting

Complete refit projects — from interior redesign to full mechanical overhauls, upgrading every system to modern standards and your specifications.

13

Docking Assistance

Professional crew assistance for berthing, mooring, marina coordination, and shore power connection in all major UAE marinas.

14

Marine Inspections

Pre-purchase surveys, insurance inspections, annual flag-state compliance checks, and hull integrity assessments by certified marine surveyors.

15

Emergency Repairs

24/7 emergency response team with a fully equipped mobile workshop. We mobilise fast to get your vessel safe and seaworthy when it matters most.
